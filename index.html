New Member Registration

Welcome to Seahawk! We're excited to have you onboard. Before joining us, please take a moment to read through the following:

1. Team Philosophy
We welcome paddlers of all levels who are passionate about the sport and eager to grow. Seahawk is a competitive team, and while we support everyone's development, selection for race crews is based on training attendance, consistent effort, and performance.

2. Training Commitment
Members are encouraged to attend training regularly to build team synergy and personal progress.

3. Code of Conduct
Treat one another with respect, foster positivity, and contribute to the team both in and out of training.

4. Health & Safety
Ensure you're medically fit to participate. Inform the team of any conditions that may affect your involvement.
